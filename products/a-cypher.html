About A-Cypher

When we need to lend our USB or comuter to the others but we don't want them to watch some of the data, or we want to express some "special" emotions and feelings but it isn't good to speak out derectly, you can try to use this kind of tool.

What Is It?

We usually see different kinds of encryption. From picture to https, cypher are evrywhere. But how can we design an easy but convenient and fast encryption? This is one of the solution.

How It Works?

1. First, we have a list to memoerize the cypher.

- 1 2 3 4 5
6 a b c d e
7 f g h i/j k
8 l m n o p
9 q r s t u
0 v w x y z

2. Then, program will read the list and put the data into the list and get a result.
3. Next, put a "0" on both font and the end.
4. After that, if you use double encryption, put the data into the list again and get the secret result. If you just use encryption, program will just split it with four object a group, and put a SPACE between groups.
