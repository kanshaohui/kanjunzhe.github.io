About Stones

From buying things from the market, to the examination paper, maths are everywhere. It lives and changes our life. So, maths games are going to come out while the maths is developing.

What Is It?

You two have some stones. And the number of the stones is odd. Then a rule come out. In one round, each one can only take 1 or 2 stones to their bag. When all the stones has been taken, if the number your stones is also odd, you will win.

Where Is Maths?

Let's have a thought. First, there's only 1 stones, and it's your turn. You can only choose to take it away. So, if that time, the number of your stones is even, you win. We can call it:

1. Even win.

Then, let's think of another case. There's 2 stones to get. You can choose to get 1 stones or both. If the number of the stones in your hand is odd, take both, you win. If the number is even, take 1, you also win. We can call it:

2. Both win.

We can think of lots of other cases like this. It's much intresting!
